About Us

Our Story


Delfligo was founded by Alex Mugaviri with a vision to revolutionize how businesses showcase their services online. Guided by a passion for innovation and creativity, Alex established Delfligo to craft websites that do more than just exist—they captivate. Our mission is to help businesses breathe life into their services, ensuring that every website we create becomes a dynamic platform that attracts and engages customers.

What We Do

At Delfligo, we specialize in designing and maintaining exceptional websites that elevate brands and drive results. Whether you’re looking to establish your online presence, refresh your current website, or enhance functionality, we’re here to deliver solutions tailored to your needs. Every website we create is a blend of stunning visuals, user-friendly functionality, and cutting-edge technology, ensuring your business stands out in today’s competitive digital landscape.

Our Philosophy

We believe that a website is more than a digital placeholder—it’s a gateway to new opportunities. That’s why we approach every project with creativity, care, and a commitment to excellence. At Delfligo, we’re not just building websites; we’re helping businesses tell their story and connect with their audience in meaningful ways.

Why Choose Delfligo?

With Delfligo, you’re choosing a partner who is as invested in your success as you are. Our team takes the time to understand your vision and translate it into a website that reflects your brand's identity. From the initial concept to ongoing maintenance, we’re here to ensure your website grows with your business and remains a powerful tool for your success.

Our Services

At Delfligo, we offer a comprehensive range of services to meet the diverse needs of our clients. From innovative mobile app development to user-focused design, our goal is to help your business thrive in the digital age.

Android Development

Delfligo specializes in creating high-performing Android applications tailored to your business needs. Whether you need an e-commerce app, a service booking platform, or a custom solution, we ensure your app is user-friendly, scalable, and optimized for success.

Apple iOS Development

Our team creates sleek and powerful iOS applications designed to captivate Apple users. From ideation to deployment, Delfligo delivers polished solutions that align with the latest Apple design standards and ensure a seamless user experience.

Web Design

We craft stunning, responsive website designs that bring your brand to life. At Delfligo, we focus on creating visually engaging and functional websites that provide exceptional user experiences and leave lasting impressions.

Concept Development

From brainstorming ideas to refining concepts, we help you shape the vision for your digital presence. Delfligo’s concept development services ensure your projects start strong with clear, innovative ideas that align with your goals.

User Research

Understanding your audience is at the core of what we do. Through detailed user research, Delfligo identifies user needs, preferences, and behaviors, enabling us to design solutions that truly resonate with your target market.

User Experience (UX) Design

Delfligo creates intuitive, seamless, and engaging user experiences. By focusing on usability, accessibility, and customer satisfaction, we design interfaces that keep users coming back and make your digital platforms a joy to interact with.
